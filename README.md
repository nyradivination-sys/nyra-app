# Nyra — AI-Powered Astrology App

> *Where the ancient meets the algorithmic.*

Nyra is a dark, editorial astrology web app that delivers real-time cosmic readings, tarot interpretations, natal birth charts, wardrobe guidance, and palmistry analysis — all powered by the Claude AI API. It's designed with a precise, unsentimental voice inspired by Co–Star, serving the modern seeker who wants substance over fluff.

---

## Features

### ☉ Cosmic Insights (Daily Astrology)
Generates 8 personalized domain readings on page load — Self, Thinking, Emotions, The Unconscious, Relationships, Work, Growth, and Spirituality — based on current planetary transits. Optionally personalizes readings using the user's saved natal chart data.

### 🃏 Tarot
Single-card pulls and 3-card past-present-future spreads. Each card reading is generated fresh by the AI with upright or reversed orientation, 3 keywords, ruling planet, and element. Available in the full app.

### ♈ Horoscope
Click any of the 12 zodiac signs to get a daily reading with Love, Work, and Body sub-sections. Readings are written to today's planetary weather, not generic sign descriptions.

### 🌐 Birth Chart
Enter a birth date, time, and place to generate a full natal chart. The app renders an SVG chart wheel, displays Big Three placements (Sun, Moon, Rising), and shows all 10 planetary positions with signs, degrees, and houses. Birth data is saved locally and used to personalize daily and wardrobe readings.

### 👗 Astro Wardrobe
Translates the day's Moon sign energy into specific wardrobe guidance — a color with hex code, fabrics, accessories, and what to avoid. Includes a 3-day timeline (yesterday, today, tomorrow). Updates with each Moon sign change.

### 🖐 Palmistry
Upload a photo of your dominant hand and receive AI-generated readings for the Heart, Head, Life, and Fate lines, plus a synthesizing narrative. Powered by Claude's vision capability. Available in the full app.

### 📚 The Library (Info)
A tabbed reference section covering Zodiac Signs, Tarot Cards, Planets & Houses, and Aspects — for users learning the language of astrology.

---
## Data & Privacy

- All user data (birth date, time, place, natal chart) is stored exclusively in **`localStorage`** under the key `stellar_birth_data`.
- No data is ever sent to a server. No accounts. No tracking.
- The Anthropic API receives only the minimal context needed for each reading (e.g., birth date + today's date for the chart agent).
- The palmistry agent is the only feature that sends image data — directly to the Anthropic API, not to any other service.

## Design Principles

- **Dark, editorial aesthetic** — black backgrounds, gold accents, Playfair Display for headings, IBM Plex for body text.
- **Declarative voice** — readings make statements, never hedges. No "you may" or "it is possible."
- **Progressive personalization** — the app works without any birth data; adding it makes readings personal.
- **Accessibility** — `aria-live` regions on all AI-updated content, semantic HTML throughout, keyboard-navigable modals.
- **No dependencies** — pure HTML/CSS/JS. No React, no bundler, no build step.
